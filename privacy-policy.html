Wind — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Wind is an offline propeller plane game. Your privacy is important to us, so we designed the app to work entirely on your device.

Data Collection

Wind does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No accounts or sign-ins are required.
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included.
  • No personal data is sent to any server (see Crash Reporting below for anonymous diagnostics).

Locally Stored Data

The app saves your best score and settings (sound, haptics, axis inversion) in local device storage (SharedPreferences). This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Crash Reporting

Wind uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports. These reports contain device model, OS version, and stack traces — no personal information. You can opt out by disabling crash data collection in your device's Google settings.

Permissions

  • VIBRATE — used for haptic feedback during gameplay.

Children's Privacy

Wind does not collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
